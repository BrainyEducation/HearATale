<b>(35-1)</b>   &nbsp; &nbsp;  Can you do it?     &nbsp; &nbsp;        Grade: K <br>
<br>
<p>What would you do if you met a giant like one that Jack met? Would you be scared and hide?  Would you be brave and try to fight.  Would you pretend you didn’t see him and just walk away?  Would you tell someone to phone 911 and call a policeman?  What did Cormoran the giant do to make Jack kill him?</p>

<b>(35-2)</b>   &nbsp; &nbsp;  Can you do it?     &nbsp; &nbsp;        Grade: K <br>
<br>
<p>Name as many different giants in the story as you can and tell one thing about each giant.</p>

<b>(35-3)</b>   &nbsp; &nbsp;  Can you do it?     &nbsp; &nbsp;        Grade: K <br>
<br>
<p>Tell three different things about Jack or about what Jack did. </p>

<b>(35-4)</b>   &nbsp; &nbsp;  Can you do it?     &nbsp; &nbsp;        Grade: K <br>
<br>
<p>Large or small:  Draw a large circle and a small circle near the top of a piece of paper. Turn the big circles into a giant’s face and turn the small circle into Jack’s face, and then draw the rest of the giant and the rest of Jack. </p>

<b>(35-5)</b>   &nbsp; &nbsp;  Can you do it?     &nbsp; &nbsp;        Grade: 1 <br>
<br>
<p>Jack had a special gift.  He could kill giants.  Tell about him killing two of the giants.  How did he rescue the knight and the lady?  After he killed the last giant, what did he give the lady?  Did you like this story?  Say why you did or why you didn’t.</p>
