<i>On the Innocents</i>
Our King is swift
To gather up the blood of the Innocents,
For which the angels sing together and proclaim their praises,
But the clouds over the same bloodshed
Lament.

Now the tyrant in the heavy sleep of death
Has suffocated because of his malice, 
But the clouds over the same bloodshed 
Lament.

Glory be to the Father and to the Son and to the Holy Spirit
But the clouds over the same bloodshed
Lament.
